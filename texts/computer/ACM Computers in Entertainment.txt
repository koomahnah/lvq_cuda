ACM Computers in Entertainment is a hybrid online academic journal and magazine that features both peer-reviewed articles as well as news content that covers entertainment technology, products, and services. It was founded in 2003 by Newton Lee and is published by the Association for Computing Machinery.



The following persons have been (co)editors-in-chief of the journal:
Newton Lee, (2003 present)
Adrian David Cheok and Masa Inakage, (2009 2011)
